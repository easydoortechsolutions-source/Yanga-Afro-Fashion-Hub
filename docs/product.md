Yanga is a marketplace that connects:
- Verified fashion vendors
- With buyers (Nigeria + diaspora)

The MVP includes:

For buyers:
- Browse listings
- View vendor profiles
- Pay securely via escrow
- Track order status
- Confirm delivery

For vendors:
- Apply and get verified
- Upload products
- Manage orders
- Get paid after buyer confirmation

For Yanga:
- Approve vendors
- Hold payments in escrow
- Resolve disputes
- Release funds

The MVP does NOT include:
- Logistics ownership
- Manufacturing
- Social networking
- Custom tailoring workflows
- AI fashion tools

Those come later.

The only thing that matters now is:
**Trust, payments, and successful transactions.**
